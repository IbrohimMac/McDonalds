Hamburger (English hamburger, abbreviated burger) is a hot dish, usually consisting of a cutlet of chopped meat, usually beef, placed inside a sliced ​​bun.

Hamburgers are often served with cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, onions, pickles, bacon, or chili; sauces such as ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, relish; and are often placed on sesame seed buns.

A hamburger topped with cheese is called a cheeseburger[1]. Hamburgers are commonly sold in fast food restaurants, snack bars, and specialty and high-end restaurants. There are many international and regional variations of hamburgers.

In May 2012, Serendipity 3 was recognized by Guinness World Records for serving the world's most expensive hamburger, the $295 Le Burger Extravagant.[22]
